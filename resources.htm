Philatelic Web Resources

Here are a few websites that we recommend.

General Philatelic Organizations

  • American Philatelic Society (APS)
    The largest nonprofit stamp collecting organization in the world.
  • Royal Philatelic Society London (RPSL)
    One of the oldest philatelic societies, known for research and expertizing.
  • American Stamp Dealers Association (ASDA)
    Great for dealer directories and show info.

Research & Reference

  • Stamp Smarter
    Free tools, articles, and a massive online library for collectors.
  • United States Stamp Society (USSS)
    Photos, history, and info on U.S. stamps.
  • Postal History Foundation
    A goldmine of historic postal material.
  • Philatelic Foundation
    Known for expertizing services and a searchable database of certificates.

Catalogs & Databases

  • StampWorld
    A huge global online stamp catalog.
  • Colnect
    Great for managing and trading collections with other collectors.

Buying & Selling

  • HipStamp
    Marketplace for stamps with a strong community focus.
  • eBay Stamps Category
    Always a mix of bargains and treasures (but buyer beware).
  • Siegels Auctions
    Premier U.S. auction house for high-end material.

Philatelic News & Blogs

  • Linn's Stamp News
    Industry news, tips, and articles.
  • The Virtual Stamp Club
    Great for updates on new issues and community chatter.
